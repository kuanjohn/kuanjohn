# AI Copilots for VMware Statements of Work

Writing a credible SOW for VMware Cloud Foundation or NSX work is slow when every engagement reinvents structure. An **AI delivery copilot** changes the economics.

## What good looks like

- Facts are confirmed and cited — the model does not invent SKUs.
- Phases follow delivery discipline: assess → deploy/migrate → UAT & KT.
- Risks, exclusions, and indicative costing stay explicit.

## Outcome

Teams spend time on customer-specific judgment, not boilerplate. That is AI as leverage for enterprise delivery — the same mindset behind AI Services Delivery.
