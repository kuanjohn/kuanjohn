# Agentic CRM Qualification for Infrastructure Sales

Pipeline is expensive. Letting every lead consume SE time is worse. An **AI qualification chat** scores intent, environment size, and urgency before outreach continues.

## Design principles

- Thresholds are explicit (e.g. handoff above a score).
- Campaigns respect daily limits and audit trails.
- Humans own the relationship; AI owns the triage.

Built into CloudiMesh CRM — AI where it reduces toil, humans where trust is earned.
