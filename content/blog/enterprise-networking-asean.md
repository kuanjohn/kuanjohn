# Enterprise Networking Lessons from ASEAN Wins

Landmark deals teach more than product slides. A **560-site SD-WAN** in banking, NSX ELAs in telco, and VCF seeding across regulated industries share a pattern: architecture clarity, partner enablement, and executive sponsorship.

## What transferred

- Start with a sharp use case, not a catalog dump.
- Enable 30+ partners as force multipliers.
- Measure outcomes in resilience and operating model — not just licenses.

Those lessons still inform how I design hybrid cloud products and advise digital transformation programs today.
